Our Services

Emergency Services

NovaCare provides immediate care for acute injuries, illnesses, and life-threatening conditions. Staffed with emergency physicians, nurses, and other healthcare professionals, this department operates 24/7 to handle emergencies such as trauma, heart attacks, strokes, and accidents

Inpatient & Outpatient Services

We offer comprehensive healthcare services providing round-the-clock care for admitted patients (inpatient) and convenient same-day treatments, diagnostics, and consultations (outpatient), ensuring seamless and personalized medical care for all needs.

Surgical Services

Our surgical services encompass preoperative, operative, and postoperative care for patients undergoing surgeries. This includes operating rooms equipped with advanced technology, skilled surgeons, anesthetists, and support staff to perform procedures ranging from minor surgeries to complex operations.

Maternity & Neonatal Services

This focuses on the care of pregnant women, childbirth, and newborn babies. It includes services such as prenatal care, labor and delivery, postnatal care, and neonatal intensive care units (NICUs) for premature or critically ill newborns.
